Privacy Policy for MyGoldWorth

Last Updated: January 13, 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to MyGoldWorth ("we," "our," or "us"). We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy explains how we handle your information when you use our mobile application.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Data Stored Locally

MyGoldWorth stores the following data locally on your device only:

  • Gold and silver portfolio items (name, weight, purity)
  • Purchase information (optional: purchase price and date)
  • Notes and metadata about your items

Important: All portfolio data is stored locally using SQLite database on your device. We do NOT collect, transmit, or store this data on our servers.

2.2 Data We Do NOT Collect

We do NOT collect:

  • Personal identification information (name, email, phone number)
  • Location data
  • Financial account information
  • Contact lists or photos
  • Any data from your device beyond what's necessary for the app to function

3. How We Use Your Information

The data stored locally on your device is used solely to:

  • Display your gold and silver portfolio
  • Calculate the current value of your items
  • Track profit/loss based on purchase prices
  • Provide portfolio statistics and insights

4. Data Sharing

We do NOT share, sell, or transfer your portfolio data to any third parties. Your data remains on your device at all times.

5. Data Security

Your portfolio data is stored securely in a local SQLite database on your device. Since the data never leaves your device, it is protected by your device's security measures (passcode, biometric authentication, etc.).

6. Data Retention and Deletion

Your portfolio data is stored on your device until you:

  • Delete items within the app
  • Uninstall the app (which removes all local data)

To delete all your data, simply uninstall the MyGoldWorth app from your device.

7. Children's Privacy

MyGoldWorth is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new privacy policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.

9. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your data (stored locally on your device)
  • Delete your data (by deleting items or uninstalling the app)
  • Opt-out of personalized ads (through device settings)

10. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us:

  • Email: Support Email
  • Support Page: MyGoldWorth Support

11. Consent

By using MyGoldWorth, you consent to this Privacy Policy and agree to its terms.
